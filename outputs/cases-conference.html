June 3-4, 2026 | Manchester

CASES Conference 2026

Michelle will be giving a presentation based on her work in progress on developing pedagogical confidence and supporting pupils with ADHD in primary PE. She will be delivering a poster presentation on Thursday 4 June in the Pedagogy section.

The poster shares research-informed reflections on how primary PE teachers develop confidence through experience, reflection, and support from the wider school system.
